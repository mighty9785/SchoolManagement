About us

The Education key of Success

Important Activities are:

  • Management of existing Government Polytechnic colleges
  • Opening of new Government Polytechnic colleges
  • Issuing temporary and permanent NOC to open new Private Polytechnic Colleges
  • Distribution of various scholarships to students of Technical Education
  • Ensuring quality Technical Education in the State

The Directorate of Technical Education was set up in 1956 for the administration and development of Technical Education in the State of Rajasthan. The department is now managing 43 Government Polytechnic Colleges (35 Co-ed & 08 Women), 108 Unaided Private Polytechnic Colleges. The administration is headed by the Director, Technical Education, Government of Rajasthan, Jodhpur.
